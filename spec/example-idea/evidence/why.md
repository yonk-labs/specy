# Why — evidence (standard intensity, ×5 dig)

- Why build it? → To stay hydrated during long desk sessions.
- Why does that need a tool? → The user reliably forgets without an external nudge.
- Why not the phone apps you have? → Wrong device; they're ignored during deep work.
- Why terminal? → That's where the user already is all day; zero context switch.
- Why now? → No terminal-native option exists, and the pain is daily.

**Bedrock:** the nudge must land in the context the user can't ignore — the terminal.
